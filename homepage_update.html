UReview is an app dedicated to ensuring the quality of university meals across Canada. Listed below are universities that allow
students to rate the meals of their residence's cafeteria and share it with their fellow scholars using this app. With UReview you
can view what your peers have rated the meals for today, and avoid brick-like waffles, desert dry salmon and baseball tough steak.
Never fear when UReview is here!
